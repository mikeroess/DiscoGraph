Welcome to DiscoGraph, a simple interactive data-visualization built with d3 and the Discogs API.

To use the graph:

  • Adjust the sliders to the years you're interested in viewing (between 1950 and 2016)
  • Click the buttons to display or hide a particular genre
  • Use the style field to look for a style* you don't see
  • Move your mouse over the graph to adjust the pie chart's year**
You can find all kind of cool information this way.
  • Did you know that in 1978 there were more releases in the funk/soul genre than pop? I didn't!
  • Also, check out the trend lines for Funk/Soul and Disco. Shockingly similar!
