DiscoGraph

Welcome to DiscoGraph, a simple interactive data-visualization built with d3 and drawing information from the Discogs API It should be fairly intuitive, but the sliders allow you to adjust the timescale of the graph. The buttons toggle which genres are displayed. If the genre you're interested in isn't present, don't fret! Just add it as a sub-genre. We'll gather the information from Discogs and throw it up for you. Please poke around and look for anything you might find interesting. I was surprised to find that in 1978 there were more releases classified as funk or soul than pop!
